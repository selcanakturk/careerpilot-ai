# Product Overview

## Product Name

CareerPilot AI

## Product Type

AI-powered Career Assistant (SaaS)

## Vision

CareerPilot AI helps job seekers understand how well their CV matches a target role and provides personalized AI-powered guidance to improve their chances of getting hired.

Instead of only reviewing a resume, the platform acts as a personal career coach by analyzing skills, identifying gaps, recommending learning paths, and generating interview preparation materials.

---

# Problem Statement

Finding a job has become increasingly competitive.

Many candidates:

- don't know if their CV is good enough
- don't know which skills are missing
- don't know how to improve their resume
- prepare for interviews inefficiently
- receive little or no feedback after applications

CareerPilot AI combines these steps into a single intelligent platform.

# Out of Scope (MVP)

The following features are intentionally excluded from version 1.0:

- LinkedIn Integration
- Cover Letter Generator
- Live AI Interview
- Voice Interview
- Recruiter Dashboard
- Mobile Application
